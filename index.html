How I became a YouTuber:

Before when I first started my YouTube channel, I mainly watched gaming videos for fun. But after spending quite some time on YouTube, I then decided to start making YouTube videos on my very own YouTube channel. That eventually led me to taking a video productions course in high school. Whenever I was in that course, I found myself spending a lot of time working on my videos even when I didn't have to go to class. I simply found myself becoming passionate towards video productions which eventually lead myself to expanding my YouTube channel to the way it currently is now.

I was so passionate with video productions, that I even made myself work on a personal project which was creating my very first gaming montage video which is also the first video that I'm showcasing on my website right here:

Super Smash Bros. is a one of my most favorite games to play. In fact, all the videos on my channel are mainly highlights of me playing online matches with random people on Super Smash Bros.

Capturing the gaming footage that I needed for my videos isn't a simple task. In fact, it's requires multiple tasks, especially if I wanted to put together a montage video to post onto my channel. I needed to use a game capture device in order to capture my gaming videos. And the game capture device that I always use for capturing my videos is an Elgato HD60.
